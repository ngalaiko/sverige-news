About this website

This website is a simple swedish news aggregator that I have built for personal use.

It works as follows:

  1. Keeps track of a list of news sources by either downloading their RSS or directly scraping websites.
  2. Generates embeddings for each of the news article using OpenAI's API. Embeddings are kind of meaning of the article encoded in location in multi-dimentional space.
  3. Groups those embeddings into clusters using DBSCAN algorithm. It is a density based clustering algorithm that groups closely located points into the same cluster.
  4. For each cluster, it selects the most representative article based on the distance to the center of the cluster.
  5. Translates grouped articles into English using OpenAI's Chat completion API.
  6. Scores each group. The score is based on the number of articles in the group and time when the article was published.
  7. Displays the most representative article title for each group along with the link to the original.
